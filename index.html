Are you in need of child care

If you are a parent or guardian receiving cash assistance from the Department of Transitional Assistance (DTA), you may qualify for free or low-cost child care. Please contact your DTA case worker and ask for a child care referral.

The Department of Early Education and Care (EEC) provides child care financial assistance for families in need.
